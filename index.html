Know Your Coffee




What is Espresso ?

+

Espresso is a concentrated coffee made by forcing hot water under high pressure (about 9 bars) through finely ground coffee beans.

What is Cappuccino ?

+

Cappuccino is a traditional Italian coffee drink prepared with a shot of espresso, hot milk, and a thick layer of milk foam on top. It is known for its rich flavor, velvety texture, and creamy finish. Typically served in a small cup, cappuccino offers a perfect balance between the boldness of espresso and the smoothness of milk.

What is Americano ?

+

An Americano is a coffee drink prepared by diluting a shot of espresso with hot water, giving it a lighter body and milder taste while preserving the rich aroma of espresso.
